Softline and Adobe held the Student UI / UX Design Championship

Maria Sedelnikova,
Product Owner at Space307
25.05.2021
From March 1 to April 21, the Adobe UI / UX design championship was organized for students of St. The official organizers were the global provider of IT solutions and services Softline, as well as the world's leading developer of software for web-design Adobe
During the competition, the participants were invited to unite in teams of 3 people and come up with ideas for IT startups that would help our world to become a better place. At the first stage, students developed a logo and design concept for their project. In the final, the contestants presented either a prototype site or a prototype of a mobile application without code) developed in the Adobe XD program. Contest entries were assessed in absentia by representatives of Adobe. The main criteria were: innovativeness of the idea, creativity, style, relevance of the chosen topic, originality
It was a real pleasure for me to get to know the prototypes that made it to the last round of the design competition. The level of work was very high and I am very impressed with how many contributors were able to make the most of the prototyping capabilities in Adobe XD
Lisa Ann Reeves,
Global Strategic Development Manager
for Adobe Design Solutions

Unite in teams of 3 people and come up with ideas for IT startups

At the first stage, students developed a logo and design concept for their project. In the final, the contestants presented either a prototype site or a prototype of a mobile application (without code) developed in the Adobe XD program. Contest entries were assessed in absentia by representatives of Adobe. The main criteria were: innovativeness of the idea, creativity, style, relevance of the chosen topic, originality

Offline performances were assessed by specially invited jury members: Experts from Softline, Space307, Distillery and Dostavista Global

Full-time defense of the project took place in the creative space of SPbGUPTD "Boiling Point - PromTechDesign" in Moscow. The Scaneco team became the winner. The students successfully defended the design project of an ecological startup, the goal of which is to simplify the process of sorting recyclable materials: by scanning the product packaging, the user will know where it can be disposed of.

The teams that took the first three places received OZON certificates for a significant amount, and the winning team was also given the opportunity to complete a 6-month internship at Distillery. Inspired by the idea of ​​the competition, experts from Space307 decided to highlight their own "Space nominations". The company presented the best teams with sets of original merchandise and souvenirs
